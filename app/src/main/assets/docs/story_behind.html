Nowsaday, many people procrastinate their task and lead to cannot complete their task on time. This phenomenon often occur in student or office worker. There some reasons caused this, such as fear of failure , distracted by their mobile phone and poor time management. For the time managment, many people does not has a habit to write down what they need to do or did not plan when start on working the task. Cedule is a task management app and aim to help people to manage their task and remind them to work it on time, as well as try to help them to focus on their task.
