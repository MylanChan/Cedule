Nowadays, many people procrastinate on their tasks and cannot complete their task on time. This phenomenon often occurs in students or office workers. Some reasons caused this, such as fear of failure, being distracted by their mobile phone, and poor time management. For time management, many people do not have a habit of writing down what they need to do or do not plan when start on working the task. Cedule is a task management app that aims to help people to manage their tasks and remind them to work on time, as well as try to help them to focus on their tasks.
